About Shift Securely

Shift Securely is a pioneering initiative dedicated to empowering developers, security practitioners, and organizations to integrate security into every phase of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) and DevOps processes. Founded on the belief that secure software is achievable without compromising agility or innovation, Shift Securely is on a mission to teach and enable the development of robust and secure applications.
